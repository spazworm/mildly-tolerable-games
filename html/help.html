Frequenty Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why can't I add items to cart
Before purchasing items you must first register and login, after which you can navigate to any product's page and click the add to cart button and follow the prompts.

What protection does this site give to software piracy
At the moment this site does not have any anti-piracy measures in place, it is the opinion of the site's owner that any Digital Rights Managment may just get in the way of people enjoying the product, especially in the case of products that require no internet connection to be played. In an ideal world we'd like to try and trust our customers to give us a fair go and if they really enjoy a product it would be great if they could make a purchase of it.

I lost my downloaded copy of a game, where can I re-download it
You can re-download any purchase at any time by logging into the site and clicking the "View Account" button on any page which will take you to a list of all products you've purchases along with download links to re-download them.

What templates were used for creating this site, where can I get them
There were no templates or design tools used in the creation of this site, the code for every style, line and feature was coded specifically for this site as a demonstration of the designer's training.
